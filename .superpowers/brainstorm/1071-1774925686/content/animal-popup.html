B: Vertical List

Full-width rows with emoji, name, description, and price inline. More room for description text. Scrollable list.

Interactions

  • Unlocked animal: Tap to equip (replaces current). Green border + EQUIPPED badge.
  • Locked + enough gems: Tap to unlock. Confirmation dialog: "Unlock Chicken for 💎 100?"
  • Locked + not enough gems: Tap shows "Need 💎 53 more" or similar. Dimmed buy button.
  • Unequip: Tap the currently equipped animal to unequip (no animal active).
