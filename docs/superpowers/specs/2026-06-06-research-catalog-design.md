# Research Catalog & Compost System — Design Spec

## Overview

The Research system lives in the **Greenhouse** building. Players assign one research to each of their unlocked slots (up to 4); each research progresses in real time, even while the game is closed, leveling up against an exponential cost + time curve. Most researches grant **multiplicative stat bonuses** that stack with equipment/upgrades. The catalog spans 7 branches and ~40 researches with three pacing tiers (binary, 10-lvl, 25-lvl, 100-lvl standard, and 100-lvl "absurd" for coin-related stats).

A second system — **Compost** — is introduced alongside Research. Compost is a farmed currency the player spends to temporarily boost a slot's progression speed (2x / 3x / 4x). Compost is generated by a new **Compost Bay** equipment placed per zone (collects all dead crops in its zone, no radius/capacity) and by **Cow** animals (passively, with an extra bonus during runs from occasional crop-eating).

The framework already exists: `ResearchManager` (slot unlock state + `OnSlotUnlocked` event), `ResearchPopupUITK` (popup shell with 4 slot cards), Greenhouse building gated by Carpenter. This spec covers the missing pieces: the data shape, the catalog, visibility rules, time + cost curves, multiplicative stacking, and the Compost system.

This spec **does not** cover offline tick infrastructure (timestamps, clock-cheat mitigation) — that is shared infra called out in the existing `project_research_system.md` memory and will get its own spec.

## Visibility Rules

A research is visible to the player only if **all of the following** are true:

1. **Game-state gate** is met (relevant equipment owned, animal owned, etc.)
2. **Prerequisite research** is completed (currently only Offline Cap + Offline Efficiency depend on Offline Unlock)

Visibility is per-research and computed at popup-open time. Hidden researches are not shown as "locked" — they simply do not appear in the list. This keeps the catalog manageable for early-game players.

Slot 3 and Slot 4 are special: the **slot-unlock researches** are always visible. The slot itself remains locked until the matching binary research completes (existing `ResearchManager` `SlotUnlockType.Research` path).

## Scaling Curves

All curves are **polynomial per level** so single-level time ramps fast through the early levels (days by L20-25) without exploding at L100. Formula:

```
duration(L) = baseDurationSecs × timeDifficulty × L^p_time
cost(L)     = baseCostCoins    × costDifficulty × L^p_cost
```

Default exponents (global, applied to all non-binary researches):
- `p_time = 2.16` — calibrated so a standard 100-lvl research lands L25 ≈ 1.5 days, L100 ≈ 30 days
- `p_cost = 2.00` — slightly gentler so costDifficulty can dial up specific researches without all of them ballooning

Each `ResearchData` carries two per-research scalars (`timeDifficulty`, `costDifficulty`, both default 1.0) so individual researches can be made painful in time, in cost, or both — without changing the global curve. Use these for balance: dial up `costDifficulty` on coin-related researches you don't want minmaxed, dial down on equipment researches you want accessible, etc.

| Tier | Time base (L1) | L25 single-lvl | L100 single-lvl | Cost base (L1) | Cumulative cost at L100 (diff=1.0) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Binary | fixed | n/a | n/a | fixed | fixed (per-research) |
| Tier10 (10-lvl) | 1 hr | n/a | n/a (max L10) | 1,000 | n/a (max L10) |
| Tier25 (25-lvl) | 5 min | ~22 hr | n/a (max L25) | 200 | ~25K @ L25 |
| Tier100 standard | 2 min | ~1.5 days | **~30 days** | 50 | ~17M |
| Tier100 absurd | 6 min | ~4.4 days | **~90 days** | 150 | ~51M |

### First-Pass Difficulty Dials

Defaults are `1.0` for both axes. Only researches listed below diverge — they reflect explicit min-max protection decisions: coin-related researches must be HARD so players can't trivialize the economy; soil and plant-quality stats must be HARD so the farm doesn't become passive money-printing; Scarecrow is allowed to come up faster because dialing it in early is a satisfying gameplay arc; powerful accelerators (Game Speed, Research Speed) are coin-gated to fit the "fast to research but too expensive to continue" pattern.

| Research | timeDifficulty | costDifficulty | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Soil: Water Efficiency | 1.5 | 2.0 | Soil perks should be HARD |
| Soil: Quality (% coins/harvest) | 1.2 | 3.0 | Coin-related + soil = double HARD |
| Helper: Harvest Efficiency ($/harvest) | 1.2 | 3.0 | Coin-related |
| Plant: Growth Speed | 1.5 | 2.0 | Plant quality/growth should be HARD |
| Plant: Bonus Sell Amount (flat $) | 1.2 | 3.0 | Coin-related, single biggest minmax target |
| Scarecrow AoE | 0.8 | 0.8 | Scarecrow allowed to max easier |
| Scarecrow Effectiveness | 0.8 | 0.8 | Scarecrow allowed to max easier |
| Scarecrow Cooldown | 0.8 | 0.8 | Scarecrow allowed to max easier |
| Meta: Game Speed Multiplier | 1.0 | 2.5 | "Fast time, expensive cost" — powerful run accelerator, coin-gated |
| Meta: Research Speed | 1.0 | 1.5 | Mild gate on the meta-accelerator |

All other researches stay at `1.0 / 1.0` for the first pass. Tune in Unity during playtest.

### Game Speed bonus curve

Game Speed Multiplier (10 lvls) uses `bonusPerLevel = 0.9` and is interpreted as **additive** to a 1x base — i.e. **L0 = 1x, L5 = 5.5x, L10 = 10x**. Only affects in-run mechanics (crop growth, helper movement, threat spawn cadence, equipment cooldowns). Does NOT affect: research timers, offline progress, animal passives.

## Multiplicative Stat Stacking

Per `project_research_system.md`, all bonus buckets multiply, never add:

```
final = base
      × (1 + equipmentBonus)
      × (1 + researchBonus)
      × (1 + buffBonus)
      × (1 − debuffPenalty)
```

A `StatComposer.Compute(statKey, baseValue)` helper is extracted **after** 3+ systems consume the same stat key. For Research V1, consumers call `ResearchManager.GetBonus(statKey)` directly and fold into their existing equipment-multiplier chain.

`GetBonus` returns the sum of `(currentLevel * bonusPerLevel)` across all researches whose `targetStatKey == statKey`. (Sum because the same player rarely has two researches feeding one key; mathematically this is the cleanest in-bucket aggregation.)

Stat keys (one per research that grants bonuses) follow `snake_case` and align with the catalog rows above — examples:
`crop_sell_value`, `crop_growth_speed`, `crop_hp`,
`helper_till_speed`, `helper_water_speed`, `helper_water_efficiency`,
`scarecrow_aoe`, `scarecrow_effectiveness`, `scarecrow_cooldown`,
`sprinkler_aoe`, `compost_bay_conversion`,
`chicken_cooldown`, `cow_compost_passive`,
`game_speed`, `research_speed`, `offline_cap`, `offline_efficiency`.

## Compost System

### Currency

`Compost` is a new global currency tracked in `CurrencyManager` alongside `Coins` and `Gems`. **No storage cap, no overflow.** Persisted in `GameData.json`.

### Generation sources

**Compost Bay (equipment)**
- One purchasable per zone, placed visually at the top corner. No collection radius — every dead crop in the same zone yields compost on death.
- Per-crop yield = `crop.tier × compostBayConversion` (where `compostBayConversion` is the Compost Bay's Conversion Efficiency stat, modulated by the equivalent research bonus).
- Available in the Market only after **Composting Basics** Meta research completes.

**Cow (animal)**
- **Passive idle rate:** Compost generated per real-world hour, scales with `cow_compost_passive` research bonus. Works while the app is closed (folded into offline tick).
- **Run-eating bonus:** During an active run, the Cow occasionally walks to a random mature crop and eats it. Each eat grants a larger lump of compost than the equivalent passive idle minute, scales with `cow_run_yield`.
- Cow's passive generation does NOT require a Compost Bay; it goes straight to the global Compost pool.

### Spending: Slot Boost Token

The player buys a **boost token** with compost. A token applies to one slot for a fixed duration:

| Multiplier | Duration | Compost cost (strawman) |
|---|---|---|
| 2x | 4 hours | 50 |
| 3x | 4 hours | 150 |
| 4x | 4 hours | 400 |
| 2x | 12 hours | 120 |
| 3x | 12 hours | 360 |
| 4x | 12 hours | 1,000 |

- Tokens are **not stackable** on the same slot — buying a new token on an already-boosted slot replaces it.
- Boost applies as a real-time multiplier to that slot's `startUtc → now` elapsed time: `effectiveElapsed = elapsedSeconds × boostMultiplier` for the portion of elapsed time inside `[startUtc, boostExpiresUtc]`.
- Boosts continue counting while offline (assuming Offline Progress is unlocked).

### Compost UI

- Compost amount displayed in the top currency bar next to coins/gems (new icon).
- The Research Popup gains a **Boost** button on each unlocked, occupied slot. Tapping opens a small modal with the boost-token table; player picks a row, currency check, applied.
